My mother and father brought this recipe back from the states when they went over to
study in the sixties! She had to translate some of the ingredients to norwegian 
ingredients. I've translated the recipe back to english using google translate, so 
it needs some tidying up. Maybe someone on GitHub can help me.

The norwegian recipe can be found below the american recipe.

It's been my favourite cake since I was born :D

=======================
American recipe:
=======================
American chocolate cake
=======================
200 g margarine (or butter)
4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla

Mix ingredients over well , I tend to melt the butter . User kitchen mixer to mix .

1.5 dl cocoa
5.5 dl flour
1 teaspoon baking powder ( a little big ts)
1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda ( no, baking soda is not the same as baking soda)
1/2 tsp salt
3 3/4 dl cold water

Have now these ingredients in the batter you made ​​over , mix the well into ( kitchen mixer ...)

Bake in cake pan on the bottom shelf of the oven for about 40-60 minutes strikkepinne / cake stick comes out completely dry ( I have used a knife that I stick into the cake , for lack of knitting needle ;) .

Cream cake:
2 dl cocoa
7 dl powdered sugar
8-10 tablespoons warm water ( depending on how thick you want the cream )
1 egg
175g butter
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla .

I tend to use coffee instead of hot water , blending brandy or what I had to find out . The amount determines the thickness of cream.

Split the cake in half and butter half the cream in the middle. The rest atop.

Norwegian recipe:
=======================
Amerikansk sjokoladekake
=======================

200 g margarin (eller smør) 
4 dl sukker 
2 egg 
1 ts vaniljesukker 

Bland ingrediensene over godt sammen, jeg pleier å smelte smøret. Bruker kjøkkenmikser til å mikse. 

1 1/2 dl kakao 
5.5 dl hvetemel 
1 ts bakepulver (litt stor ts) 
1 1/4 ts natron (nei, natron er ikke det samme som bakepulver) 
1/2 ts salt 
3 3/4 dl kaldt vann 

Ha nå disse ingrediensene i røren du lagde over, bland de godt inn (kjøkkenmikser...) 

Stek i kakeform på nederste rille i stekeovnen i ca 40-60 minutter til strikkepinne/kakepinne kommer ut helt tørr (jeg har brukt en kniv som jeg stikker inn i kaken, i mangel av strikkepinne;). 

Krem til kaken: 
2 dl kakao 
7 dl melis 
8-10 ss varmt vann (etter hvor tykk du vil ha kremen) 
1 egg 
175 g smør
1 1/2 ts vaniljesukker. 

Jeg pleier å bruke kaffe istedetfor varmt vann, og blander konjakk eller hva jeg måtte finne på inn. Mengden bestemmer tykkelsen på kremen. 

Del kaken i to og smør halvparten av kremen i midten. Resten oppå.
